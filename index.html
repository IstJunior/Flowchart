Title

"Development of Makani Platform for Gastronomic Order Management"

Description of the Theme

Focuses on understanding customer preferences in gastronomy.

Aims to develop the Makani platform for order management in restaurants.

Objectives

General Objective

Design and develop the Makani platform to personalize restaurant offerings based on customer preferences.

Specific Objectives

Conduct surveys and interviews to gather customer preference data.

Design a customized database to store preference information.

Develop and test the Makani platform.

Delimitation

Inclusions

Research of customer preferences in the gastronomy sector.

Development of the Makani platform.

Exclusions

Management of food supply chain.

Implementation of hardware in restaurants.

Justification

To provide a technological solution for restaurants to better serve customer preferences.

Enhance customer experience, increase sales, and build customer loyalty.

Utilize research methods (surveys and interviews) to obtain precise customer preference data.

Available Resources

Research team with expertise in market research.

Tools and technologies for online surveys and data collection.

Collaboration with local restaurants for data collection.

Financial resources allocated for platform development and research.
